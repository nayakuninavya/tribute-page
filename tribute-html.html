Steve Jobs

1955-2011

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works"

steven paul jobs (February 24, 1955 - october -5, 2011) was an American entrepreneur, industrial designer, bussiness magnete, media proprietor, and investor. He was the co-founder,chaiman, and CEO of Apple; the chairman majority shareholder of pixar; a member of the Walt Disney Company's board of directors following its acquisition of pixar; and the founder, chairman ,and CEO of NeXT. He is widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, along with his early business partner and fellow Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.
